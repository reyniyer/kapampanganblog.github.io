Kapampangan, also known as Pampango, is a Filipino ethnolinguistic community that lives primarily in the central plains of Luzon, especially in the province of Pampanga, but also in parts of neighboring provinces. In the early twenty-first century, the Kapampangans numbered about two million people. Kapampangan people are proud of their race, many Kapampangan are now well-known in different industries.
